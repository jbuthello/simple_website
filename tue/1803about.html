Shipping and delivery information

Shipping and Delivery Information:
We want to ensure that your stationary items reach you in a timely and efficient manner. Here is some information about our shipping and delivery options:

Standard Shipping: We offer standard shipping for all orders, which typically takes 3-7 business days to arrive after the order is processed. Standard shipping is free for all orders over $25, and a flat rate of $5.99 for orders under $25.

Expedited Shipping:
If you need your stationary items sooner, we offer expedited shipping for an additional fee. Expedited shipping typically takes 1-3 business days to arrive after the order is processed. The cost of expedited shipping varies depending on the size of the order and the delivery location.

Processing Time: Please note that all orders require a processing time of 1-2 business days before they are shipped. Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with tracking information so you can track the delivery of your package.


International Shipping: We currently do not offer international shipping. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.
Delivery Times: Delivery times may vary depending on your location, weather conditions, and other factors outside of our control. Please note that during peak holiday seasons, delivery times may be longer than usual due to high volume.
If you have any questions or concerns about your order, please contact our customer service team for assistance. Thank you for shopping with us!

Return and exchange policies

Return Policy:
If you have bought stationary and you want to return it, you will generally need to provide proof of purchase such as a receipt or invoice.
The items must also be in their original condition and packaging. Some of our stores may offer a time limit for returns, such as 30 or 60 days from the date of purchase.
Exchange Policy:
If you have bought stationary and you want to exchange it for a different item, you will need to provide proof of purchase and the items must be in their original condition and packaging. Some of our stores do have a time limit for exchanges as well.
Refund Policy:
If you are eligible for a refund, the store may offer a refund to the original form of payment, or offer store credit instead. Some stores may charge a restocking fee for returns.

It's always a good idea to check the return and exchange policy of the store from whenever you are puchasing the product before payment. If you have any questions or concerns, don't hesitate to contact the our store's customer service for assistance.

Product information and features

Sizes: Diaries, journals, and planners can come in various sizes such as pocket-sized, A5, A4, and larger sizes.
Pocket-sized options are compact and convenient for carrying around, while larger sizes provide more space for writing and organizing.
Formats:
These products may come in different formats such as daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly.
Daily formats typically provide a page for each day, while weekly formats provide a page for each week.
Monthly formats provide an overview of each month, and yearly formats provide an overview of the entire year.
Paper quality:
The paper quality of diaries, journals, and planners can also vary.
Some products may use high-quality paper that is thick and smooth, while others may use thinner, more lightweight paper.
Paper quality can impact the writing experience and how well the ink from pens or markers is absorbed.
Other features: Some diaries, journals, and planners may have additional features such as bookmarks, pen holders, elastic closures, and built-in calendars.
Some products may also be customizable with the option to add personalization, such as names or monograms.

It's always a good idea to check the product description or ask a store representative for more information on the specific features and specifications of their diaries, journals, and planners..
